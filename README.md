💡Decoding Customer Behavior 📊

🔬I recently completed a Customer Behavior Dashboard to analyze purchasing patterns and demographics. By visualizing key metrics, we can better understand what drives revenue and how different segments interact with the brand.
Key Takeaways:🔑
Total Reach: Analyzing a base of 627 customers with an average purchase value of $60.73.
Top Performer: The Clothing category is the clear leader in both volume and revenue.
Target Demographic: Young Adults are the highest revenue contributors, followed closely by the Senior group.
Customer Satisfaction: Maintaining a solid average rating of 3.77.
